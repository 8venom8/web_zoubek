Sewing Services

Sewing services are an addition to our offer of home textiles. We will be happy to customize your curtains, sew curtains, pillows and similar accessories to order. We will also make you tablecloths and table setting according to your design. If you like cloth bags, covers, aprons and anything else from our workshop and would like to have a different color combination sewn, for example, we will make them for you in a short time.

Come with us to make your home more beautiful!
